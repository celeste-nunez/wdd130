Overview

Purpose

To create a public source of information to entice and educate potential customers, thereby building revenue.

Audience

Potential customers seeking pricing and scheduling: the site must include an informative section. Handicap customers looking to see what accommodations we offer or parents looking to bring young children: the site must include the different rafting tours we offer and information about their difficulty. Tourists interested in adventure: the site needs to offer an appealing visual. The site must be accessible through all forms of devices.
